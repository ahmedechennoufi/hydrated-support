‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app calculate my daily water goal?
The app uses scientifically-based formulas from the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) and Institute of Medicine guidelines. It considers your sex, age, weight, activity level, and climate to provide personalized recommendations. The base calculation is approximately 35ml per kg of body weight for men and 31ml per kg for women, adjusted for various factors.
Can I track beverages other than water?
Yes! The app supports tracking various beverages including tea, coffee, juice, and sodas. Each beverage type has a different water content percentage that's automatically calculated to contribute to your daily hydration goal.
How do I set up reminders?
Go to Settings and enable reminders. You can customize the reminder interval and the app will send notifications to help you maintain consistent hydration throughout the day.
What are the Premium Plans features?
Premium Plans offer specialized beverage recommendations for specific health and wellness goals including weight management, stress relief, better sleep, and fitness performance. These include traditional herbal teas and natural beverages with their historical uses and benefits.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage systems. We don't collect or transmit your personal hydration data to external servers.
Can I change my profile information?
Absolutely! Go to Settings ‚Üí Personal Profile ‚Üí Edit to update your age, weight, height, activity level, and other factors. The app will automatically recalculate your daily goal based on the updated information.
Why isn't my daily goal updating?
Make sure your profile information is complete and accurate. If you've recently updated your profile, try going to Settings and tapping "Use Recommended Goal" to apply the new calculation. Restart the app if the issue persists.
How do I delete or edit an entry?
In the History view, you can see all your logged beverages. Tap on any entry to view details or remove it from your daily total.

If the app is running slowly or crashing:

  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have the latest version installed from the App Store
  • Free up storage space on your device

Notifications Not Working

If you're not receiving hydration reminders:

  • Check that notifications are enabled in Settings ‚Üí Reminders
  • Verify notification permissions in iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Hydrated
  • Ensure Do Not Disturb mode is not blocking app notifications

Data Sync Issues

If your data seems incorrect:

  • Check that your profile information is accurate
  • Verify that entries are being logged for the correct date
  • Review your beverage types and amounts for accuracy
